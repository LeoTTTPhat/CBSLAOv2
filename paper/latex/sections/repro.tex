\section*{Reproducibility Statement}
\label{sec:repro}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Reproducibility Statement}

All code, synthetic workload generators, random seeds, and aggregated
result tables are released at \emph{(anonymous repository link to be
inserted upon submission)}. The simulator runs to completion on a
single laptop CPU in under three minutes at the default sweep.
Reported Table~\ref{tab:headline} numbers are from
\texttt{out/results\_stronger.csv} ($3{,}960$ rows), generated by
\texttt{python3 code/cbslao\_sim.py out 20 stronger results\_stronger.csv}.
The legacy $7$-policy sweep in \texttt{out/results.csv} ($3{,}780$
rows) is retained only for comparison. Plots and tables are
regenerated by
\texttt{python3 code/analyze.py out/results\_stronger.csv plots/}.
